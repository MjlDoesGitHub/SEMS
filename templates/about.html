About

Smart Environmental Monitoring System (SEMS) is a modular system that utilizes various sensors to monitor environmental factors like air quality, radiation levels, light intensity, and temperature, and relays this data to a user-friendly app interface in real-time.

This product will potentially be used in various healthcare applications, such as operation rooms, biotech labs, dentistry clinics, medical imaging centers, and other locations that pose similar needs in regards to such as air quality and radiation detection. In addition to the physical model that will be created, this project will also have a website that our group will be creating using front-end development tools, starting with designing and developing the user interface with React.JS. The backend portion of our project will have a large emphasis on data analysis from our sensors to offer real-time information and recommendations.
